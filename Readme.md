## 🎯 **Problem Statement**

> *"Scammers don't rely on technology — they rely on human weakness."*

Every day, millions fall victim to scam calls, phishing SMS, and fraudulent WhatsApp messages. Traditional solutions only detect **spam numbers** or **fake links** — **after** the damage is done.

**CallShield AI** detects the **MANIPULATION PATTERN** in real-time and alerts users **BEFORE** they get scammed.
